Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

Welcome to Tez VPN! This Privacy Policy explains how Tez VPN ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Tez VPN is designed with privacy in mind and follows a strict no-logs policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Tez VPN is a privacy-focused service. We do not collect or store any of the following:

  • No browsing history
  • No DNS queries
  • No connection logs (no timestamps, session duration, or assigned IP)
  • No online activities
  • No IP addresses (neither your original IP nor the IP assigned by the VPN)
  • No personal identifiers such as name, email, phone number

2. Information We Collect Automatically

To operate the App smoothly, we may collect minimal anonymous technical information:

  • Device Information: Anonymous device model, OS version.
  • Crash Data: Through Firebase Crashlytics for detecting and correcting app issues. Crash reports do not contain personal data.
  • App Usage Events: Basic anonymous data (e.g., VPN started/stopped) to improve service performance.

No Data Leaves Your Device

Tez VPN processes DNS and network rules locally on your device through Android's VpnService. We do not redirect traffic to external servers — Tez VPN is a local DNS-style tunnel.

3. How We Use the Information

The limited anonymous information we collect is used solely for:

  • Ensuring the App functions correctly
  • Diagnosing bugs and crashes
  • Improving performance and stability

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party SDKs:

  • Google AdMob: To display ads. AdMob may collect anonymous ad-performance data.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To diagnose technical issues.

These services may collect anonymous identifiers per their own privacy policies. Tez VPN does not share user data with them beyond what their SDKs collect automatically.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. Since Tez VPN does not store logs or personal data, the risk of exposure is minimal.

6. Children's Privacy

Tez VPN is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When changes are made, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of the document.
